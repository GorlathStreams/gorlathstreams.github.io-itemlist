Their fluffy legs grant them great speed and an evasive edge in combat, but buns tend to be more prone to stress. ### Cutebold The Cutebold is a small, dog-eared kobold-like creature. They are resistant to toxic and psychic phenomena as well as being good miners and hunters, but are easily suppressed in combat. ### Destrier A Destrier is a female Epona warhorse. They are much more fearsome in melee combat than the smaller epona, but exaggerate the general downsides of Eponakind. ### Drow A Drow is a dark-skinned elf born in the depths of the Underdark. Drow prefer ranged combat to melee. They are resistant to toxins, but are more susceptible to psychic phenomena. ### Dwarf A Dwarf is a short, stocky and stubborn humanoid. Preferring to live underground, Dwarves are resistant to heat and disease, and get a strong bonus to mining. ### Elf An Elf is a long-eared humanoid long thought to be immortal. Elves rarely eat, are nigh immune to disease, and move swift as the wind. Their elf-eyes can see far, letting their arrows fly true. ### Epona An Epona is akin to a centaur. As a one-man cavalry, epona are fast and strong melee fighters. However, they sleep a lot, work slowly and are especially vulnerable to disease. ### Half-Elf A human with elf blood, Half-Elves inherit several benefits of their Elven kin - They can move fast, see far and many find that they rarely grow hungry. ### Hobbit Hobbits are small, friendly little humanoids with a passion for merrymaking. They gain a strong bonus to their social skills and are resistant to disease. They're also resistant to stress and pain. ### Kurin The Kurin is a beastman with fox ears and three bushy tails. While they aren't so competent in combat, they happen to be avid socialites and great cooks. ### Ratkin The Ratkin is a beastman that takes on the properties of a rat. Their stature is average in combat, but they boast resistance to disease and toxins. They're generally not so social, but are good at digging, hunting and farming. ### Uniponen The Uniponen is a male Epona unicorn. While elusive in combat, they aren't so good with weapons, and prefer to be on the backlines performing medical tasks, which they excel at. {%- endif -%}
