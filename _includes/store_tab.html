Outline of each race:

For reference, each of these will be in context of being compared to a baseline human.

Android

Androids are synthetic humanoids with clearly robotic visual traits. A handcrafted one is typically more powerful than a store-bought one, though.

Battle Droid

Battle Droids are mindless combat drones. They are powerful, but are built purely for battle.

Droid

Droids are mindless general-purpose drones intended for labor. They can't do complex work, but are cheap to build and easily replacable.

Moyo

Moyo are a strange race of blueish slug people. They're slow and sturdy, and rumor has it their blood is quite the drug...

Nyaron

Nyaron are genetically engineered humans, enhanced with catlike traits including ears and a tail. They're strong-willed and agile, but sleep a lot and work slowly.

Paniel

Paniel are cyborgs with a steampunk-esque theme. Each paniel is different, possessing skills depending on their model. Paniel require a steady supply of a special fuel to survive.

Rabbie

Rabbie are genetically engineered humans, enhanced with floppy bunny ears and small, slender bodies. Rabbie are intelligent but constantly nervous and jumpy, and require a special drug known as Planetarium to survive.
