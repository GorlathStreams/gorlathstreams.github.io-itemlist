Outline of each race:

For reference, each of these will be in context of being compared to a baseline human.

Android

Androids are synthetic humanoids with clearly robotic visual traits. A handcrafted one is typically more powerful than a store-bought one, though, so if you want a good android just ask :)

Moyo

Moyo are a strange race of blueish slug people. They're slow and sturdy, and rumor has it their blood is quite the drug...

Red Moyo

A variant subspecies of the Moyo, the Red Moyo's blood has slightly different properties. Notably, the Red Moyo are almost completely immune to psychic phenomena.

Rabbie

Rabbie are genetically engineered humans, enhanced with floppy bunny ears and small, slender bodies. Rabbie are intelligent but constantly nervous and jumpy, and require a special drug known as Planetarium to survive.

Kurin

A race of pretty genetically engineered humans, possessing foxlike characteristics. A mostly female race, Kurin are highly sociable creatures, but aren't much good at combat.

Ratkin

A cute race of humans genetically engineered to be smaller and possess ratlike traits. Ratkin are notoriously inventive and like frilly things, but aren't very strong.
